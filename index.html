Model Formulas (Transparency)

Quality Attribute Scoring

The core attributes are evaluated out of 100%, calculated as a base score of 100 minus specific, weighted penalties discovered during profiling.

  • Data Integrity: 100 - (MissingRate × 120) - (DuplicateRowRate × 85) - (InvalidNumericRate × 80)
  • Accuracy: 100 - (ScoreDeviationRate × 90) - (OutlierRate × 60)
  • Transparency: 48 + (TransparencyFieldCount × 10) - (MissingRate × 35)
  • Reliability: 100 - (LinkageErrorRate × 95) - (DuplicateSupplierRate × 60)
  • Robustness: 100 - (MissingRate × 75) - (OutlierRate × 85)

Composite QA Score

A weighted sum of the five quality attributes based on user-defined configurations (default distribution below):

QA_Comp = (Integrity × w1) + (Accuracy × w2) + (Transparency × w3) + (Reliability × w4) + (Robustness × w5)

Quality Gate Control

Hard limits applied at transitional phases (e.g., Tender → Award). If the weighted score of specific attributes falls below the configured threshold, the gate fails and the process is halted pending remediation.

Potential Future Extension Modules

The following modules represent the enterprise roadmap and can be integrated with this core QA framework using the current architectural pattern.

  • ■ Supplier Quality Module: Continuous scoring of supplier financial and operational health.
  • ■ Tender Evaluation QA: Deep analysis of AI bid ranking fairness.
  • ■ Contract Award Validation: Compliance-check gating before award finalization.
  • ■ Compliance Monitoring: Automated checking against regulatory blacklists.
  • ■ KPI Drift Detection: Time-series analysis of performance drops.
  • ■ Supplier Risk Scoring: Aggregated risk index per supplier across domains.
  • ■ Procurement Fraud Red Flags: Behavioral analysis for cartel or duplicate bidding.
  • ■ ESG & Circular Procurement: Sustainability tracking against supplier records.
  • ■ Cost Performance Comparison: Baseline vs actual executed costs.
  • ■ Contract Execution Monitoring: SLAs and delivery milestone tracking.
